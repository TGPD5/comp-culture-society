L'Intendant's Ledger

Paris, 1642.

You are Émile, a weary clerk under the employment of the French State. The Royal Tax Ledger is a mess, and the King's executioner is growing restless.

Your colleague Etienne's son, Blaise, has sold you a "Pascaline"—a miracle of brass and gears—for the princely sum of 100,000 livres. It automates the carry, making your accountant life much easier, but 17th-century metallurgy is unreliable, and your Pascaline often jams.

  • The Task: Add the Tax Bills to the total using the wheels. You have 60 seconds to start. Successful additions add 7 seconds to your time limit. Unsuccessful additions deduct 5 seconds.
  • The Friction: The machine may JAM. Jiggle your mouse to fix it.
  • The Memory: Use the white boxes underneath each wheel to track your clicks for each bill.
