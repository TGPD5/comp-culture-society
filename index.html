BioCPU

Neural Architecture Interface

You are a Neural Architect. You sit in the Control Room of a human body, receiving high-level directives from the Conscious Mind above — and translating them into the precise sequence of low-level physical commands that the body's actuators can understand.

As Charles Babbage once reduced the labour of arithmetic to the clockwork motion of brass gears, you reduce intention to instruction. Every thought, a program. Every movement, a subroutine.

How to play

  • A directive arrives. Read each step's description in the instructions.
  • Find the matching machine command on the control panel and press it in order.
  • Correct on first try: +10 seconds   Correct after a hint: +7 seconds
  • First wrong answer: −4 seconds   Each subsequent wrong: −2 seconds
